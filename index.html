The Intention Log

I built this because I needed somewhere to record what I was calling in — not a notes app, not a spreadsheet. Something that felt like it took the work seriously.

This is not a manifestation gimmick. It is a record of proof. A place to write down what you set your heart toward, track how it arrived, and reflect honestly on the journey between setting and receiving.

Every entry lives only on your device. No cloud. No account. No one can see what you write — not even me.

It closes, as I close every piece of work I do, with a prayer of forgiveness and gratitude. Because intention without integrity is just wishing.

I'm sorry. Please forgive me.
Thank you. I love you.
— Vuja Tsoaele
